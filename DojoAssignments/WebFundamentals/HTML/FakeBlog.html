My Favorite TV Shows: The Vampire Diaries

Vampire Diaries is one of my all time favorite show. It is about vampires as predicted from the title. Revolves around a girl who falls for a vampire guy and builds on that. Unlike normal cheesy vampire movies and shows, this show also centers around the importance of family and friendship.
